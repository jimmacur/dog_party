A Site About Some Dogs

How To Dog

  1. Wake up your human at an unreasonable hour with the subtlety of a freight train.
  2. Demand breakfast, but act completely unimpressed once it's served.
  3. Engage in a high-speed chase with an invisible squirrel, knocking over anything in your path.
  4. End the day by barking at absolutely nothing—because it's important to keep your human on their toes.
  5. Congratulations, you've mastered the art of being a dog!

What Is Dogs

Dogs are essentially furry four-legged bundles of joy that specialize in looking adorable  while causing chaos. Whether they're chasing their tails, pretending  they don't understand the word “bath,” or giving you that award-winning   puppy dog face when you're eating, dogs are the undisputed masters of both   unconditional love and strategic mischief. In short, dogs are the world's most   lovable tricksters, always ready to turn your day upside down—and make you   laugh while doing it.

Facts From Dog

Greetings, human! As a certified expert in the field of being awesome, I'm   here to share some little-known doggy facts. First, did you know that every   belly rub adds five minutes to a dog's happiness meter? It's science. Second,   the reason we bark at the mailman is purely to remind him who's boss—spoiler:   it's us. And finally, naps are the key to our wisdom. So, if you   ever see us snoozing, just know we're deep in thought… about dinner.
